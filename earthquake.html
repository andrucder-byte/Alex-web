Earthquake in Dorohoi Lasted 5 Seconds

I felt a short but intense earthquake in Dorohoi that lasted around 5 seconds. The shaking was quick, but enough for people to stop what they were doing and check on each other.

What happened

The vibration came suddenly, moved objects lightly, and then ended fast. Most people around me stepped away from shelves and windows, then waited outside for a few minutes.

My takeaway

Even short earthquakes are a reminder to stay prepared. A small emergency kit and a calm response make a big difference.
