Driving to Romania by Car

This trip was long, but really worth it. I planned the route, checked the weather, and made sure the car was ready before leaving. It was really hot but it was fine. I got too see my grandparents, I loved it.

On the road

I made short stops for fuel, food, and rest every few hours. The road views were great, and the drive felt much easier with good planning.

What I learned

For long car trips, preparation is everything: documents, route backup, and enough breaks to stay focused while driving.
